Jamal Musiala

Jamal Musiala (born 26 February 2003) is a German professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for Bundesliga club Bayern Munich and the Germany national team. Known for his agility, dribbling, and vision, he is considered one of the most promising young players in football.

Musiala was born in Stuttgart, Germany, and moved to England at a young age. He joined Chelsea's youth academy before returning to Germany to sign with Bayern Munich. Musiala quickly rose through the ranks and became Bayern's youngest-ever goalscorer in the Bundesliga at the age of 17.

Beyond his club career, Musiala chose to represent Germany at the senior international level, making his debut in 2021. With his ability to navigate tight spaces and create scoring opportunities, Musiala is a key asset for both his club and national team.
